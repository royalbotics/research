Loyal Adhesive

Loyal Adhesive is a thermoplastic compound designed for clean, tool-free product sealing. It replaces traditional screws — enabling sleek design and easy restoration with the RPen.

Why We Use It

Instead of hiding components behind screws and glue traps, we use Loyal Adhesive to bond our products in a way that's secure — but reversible. It allows our devices to be opened, repaired, and resealed at home — even by those without technical experience.

This approach keeps devices serviceable and repair affordable — so you're never locked out of your own tools.

Key Benefits

  • Reversible: Bonds can be undone and reapplied cleanly using the RPen
  • Minimal Hardware: No screws, clips, or adhesives that damage components
  • User-Friendly: Repairable by anyone — no tech background needed
  • Product Integrity: Maintains a sealed, seamless exterior

How It Works

Each unit is sealed with custom-melted Loyal Adhesive. To open, gently heat the bond using the RPen. When resealing, a new pellet is placed on the pad, melted, and applied to rejoin the housing.
